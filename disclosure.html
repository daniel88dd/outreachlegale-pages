Affiliate Disclosure

Effective date: January 1, 2025

Short version: OutreachGo earns a commission when you click our links and make a purchase. We only recommend products we genuinely believe in — and your price is never affected.

What is an Affiliate Relationship?

OutreachGo participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means we may receive a commission when you click on certain links on this website and subsequently make a purchase or sign up for a service.

How This Affects You

Affiliate commissions never affect the price you pay. The cost of any product or service remains identical whether you access it through our links or go directly to the provider's website.

Our editorial content is written independently. We do not accept payment from brands to feature or favorably rank their products. We only list wellness products we have reviewed and believe may offer genuine value to readers looking for dental, mobility, or metabolic support.

Which Links Are Affiliate Links?

All product buttons and links on this website that direct to third-party product pages are affiliate links. At the moment, these links may send you directly to the provider's website. If you make a purchase through one of these links, we may receive a commission from the partner program.

Our Editorial Commitment

We are committed to honesty and transparency. If our assessment of a product changes, we update or remove the listing. We do not allow commission potential to drive our recommendations. Products are evaluated on formulation quality, value, brand transparency, user feedback, and fit for common wellness goals.

Legal Basis

This disclosure is made in accordance with the FTC's guidelines concerning the use of endorsements and testimonials in advertising (16 CFR § 255). For EU residents, this disclosure also aligns with Directive 2005/29/EC on unfair commercial practices.

Questions?

If you have questions about our affiliate relationships or editorial policies, see our Privacy Policy or use our data request page.
