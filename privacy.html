Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025  ·  Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Who We Are

OutreachGo ("we," "us," "our") operates this website as an independently managed affiliate content platform focused on dental, mobility, and wellness recommendations.

2. What Data We Collect

When you visit our site or click a product link, we may collect:

  • Click events (which offer link was clicked, timestamp)
  • Referral parameters or affiliate attribution details when present
  • Approximate geographic region (country/region level, not precise location)
  • Browser type and device type (for analytics purposes)
  • IP address (temporarily, for security and fraud prevention)

We do not collect names, email addresses, or payment information. We do not require any account creation.

3. How We Use Data

Collected data is used to:

  • Track affiliate click attribution for commission purposes
  • Improve our content and offer selection
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Understand aggregate traffic patterns

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising targeting. We do not profile individual users.

4. Third-Party Services

When you click a product link, you may be sent directly to a third-party website. That platform has its own privacy policy and data practices, which are independent of ours. We encourage you to review their policies before providing personal information.

Our site may use GitHub Pages, hosting providers, analytics, or content delivery services to deliver the website reliably. Those providers may process technical request data such as IP address, browser details, and request logs as part of normal site operation.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses minimal cookies, if any, and may include standard technical logs or URL parameters needed for analytics, performance, or affiliate attribution. We do not intentionally set persistent advertising cookies ourselves from the site content in these static pages.

6. Data Retention

Technical logs, analytics events, and affiliate attribution data may be retained for operational, security, and reporting purposes for a limited period, after which they may be deleted or aggregated.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, visit our Data Deletion page.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related requests, please use our Data Deletion form. For other inquiries, you may contact us through the method made available on this site or by using our data request page.
